Liquid Silhouette (Exhibition)

Exhibition Concept, 8x11.5", 2024, Adobe Illustrator

The Exhibition concept complementing the Liquid Silhouette video. A projector portrays a viewer's shadow on a wall to be seen, where motion sensors will detect movement and alter friction among the waterfall in a nearby installation. It is intended to show our direct impact on water, by changing the waterfall's descent pattern with our literal movements.
