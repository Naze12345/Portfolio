King Bob

3d Fantasy Model, 2024, Blender, Adobe Substance Painter

This render is of a fantasy character bluntly dubbed "King Bob." He represents my childhood love for games and fantasy worlds I adored when I was younger. In a way, he exists as a memento of my younger days and my aspirations to be in worlds akin to Bob's.
