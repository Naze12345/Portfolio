Hall of Mirrors

Exhibition Concept, 2024, Adobe Illustator

This is an exhibition concept designe to challenge the viewer's "perception" of perception. Different colors, perspectives, and videos are projected onto each side of the room to create confusion through one-way Mirrors. This is intended for each person to interpret differently, and in their own way based on what their eyes see.
