This is a Screen

Tool: Adobe Illustrator

This is a Screen is a multi-layered composition of various tablets, parchments, papers, flags, screens, and posters that all have the phrase "This is a Screen" written on them in various different typographies. It is meant to signify the meaning of the word "screen" goes beyond the bounds of computers, and is in fact applicable to anything we see; as a screen is simply a means for our eyes to view someting. In the physical computer screen, there are two layers of "screens": each bearing a size in comparison to how old they would have been used in scripture. The entire piece was composed in Adobe illustrator, using hundreds of Stock assets.

One portion of the final project for Art 3: Medium and Message
