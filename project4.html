This is a Screen

Digital Art, 11.5x8", 2024, Adobe Illustrator

This is a digital art piece designed to point out what has served the purpose as a "screen" over time, or in other words, a way for us to view information. The image has depth down to the smallest pixel on the computer screen that contains slates, parchments, and posters that all say "This is a Screen." As time goes on, the larger the "Screen" gets. Valuing how far we have come as a species technology wise is important, and this piece is an ode to how we started.
