Spastic Trees

Glitch Art, 8x11.5", 2023, Adobe Photoshop and Notepad++

This is a Glitch art that was made using various portions of a single image of trees. The original image was altered many times by changing its code in Notepad. A rudimentary but simple piece designed to emphasize how fractured our forests are becoming, especially amongst the constant forest fires caused by global warming.
