Dangers of Deforestation

Posters, 8x11.5", 2024, Adobe Illustrator

These brochures were designed as aesthetically pleasing infographics about deforestation. They each hope to garner the viewer's attention in a different way through imagery and color. As a proponent of enviornmental health, this was one of my most worked on projects this past year, it reflects my love for this planet and therefore my concern for it.
